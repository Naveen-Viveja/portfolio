Statistical analysis on success rate analysis of Tollywood movies


The project focuses on the statistical analysis of Tollywood movie success rates, specifically examining the impact of lead cast combinations and actor-director pairings on box office performance. By analyzing historical data on films, the project explores how different lead actor combinations and director-actor collaborations influence a movie's success. Statistical methods, including regression analysis and correlation testing, are used to determine the relationship between these factors and box office revenue. The goal is to uncover trends that can guide filmmakers and producers in making strategic decisions regarding casting and director selection to maximize the success of their films.

Time series analysis on Dengue fever and mortality checking


The project focuses on time series analysis of dengue fever data to identify patterns, trends, and seasonal variations in infection rates. By analyzing historical data, the project aims to forecast future outbreaks, enabling better preparedness and response strategies. The study explores factors such as climate, geographic location, and population density to understand their impact on the spread of dengue. Through statistical modeling and data visualization, the project aims to provide actionable insights for public health authorities to mitigate the effects of dengue and improve early warning systems for future outbreaks.
